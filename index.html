ABOUT ME


Background Education Social Media Photo Gallery Résumé
Hi, my name is Brandon Bornkamp, and I am currently a senior at Trine University in Angola, IN pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering with minors in robotics and mathematics. I was born in El Paso, TX on February 10, 1997 (23 years of age) but have lived in Fort Wayne, IN for the majority of my life. I attended Carroll High School and graduated in the top 5% of my class with a Core 40 with Academic Honors diploma back in June 2015. Following high school, I spent two years playing junior hockey--one season with the Evansville Jr. Thunderbolts, and another with the St. Louis Jr. Blues. During that time, I lived with host families, worked part-time, developed my hockey skills, trained in the gym, and traveled across the country to play against other teams and get exposed to college and higher-level junior hockey scouts. Eventually, I was received an offer from Trine University and figured that would be a great option being so close to home and having a great engineering program. With one more semester to go before graduation in May 2021, I am looking to secure a full-time electronics design position with a company in Fort Wayne, and I could not be more excited to see what the future has in store for me.
Trine University (Aug. 2017 - present)
Trine University is a private university located in Angola, Indiana. It offers degrees in the arts and sciences, business, education, and engineering. Trine was founded in 1884 as Tri-State Normal College and retained the reference to the "tri-state" area for more than 120 years because of its location in Indiana and proximity to Michigan and Ohio. In 1906, the school was renamed Tri-State College, and in 1975 Tri-State University. In 2008, the school's name was changed to the Trine University, in honor of alumnus Dr. Ralph Trine and his wife Sheri. I received an offer to play hockey and attend Trine in 2017, following a two-year junior hockey stint. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering, along with minors in robotics and mathematics.

Eleven Fifty Academy: Winter Coding Experience (Dec. 2020 - present)
Eleven Fifty Academy is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit code and cybersecurity academy and was the first coding bootcamp in Indiana. The mission of Eleven Fifty Academy is to build an ecosystem of tech talent that financially benefits the individual, their employer, and their community. Their strength originates in the diversity of students, and their passion is helping individuals create meaningful impacts in their lives through finding their highest purpose. I am currenty partaking in the Eleven Fifty Academy Winter Coding Experience and am learning how to use HTML, CSS, Javascript and Python in various applications. Upon completion of the course, I will receive a badge coding certification to put on my LinkedIn profile.

Carroll High School (Aug. 2011 - Jun. 2015)
Carroll High School is a public highschool in Fort Wayne, Indiana. It is part of the Northwest Allen County Schools district. The school opened in 1969 and constructed additions to the facility in 1992 and 1996. A freshman campus was created in 2005. I took several PLTW (Project Lead the Way) courses that were engineering/STEM focused and also was a member of the soccer, hockey, and track teams. Furthermore, I was a National Honors Society member and graduated in the top 5% of my class, going on to eventually receive my Core 40 with Academic Honors Diploma in June 2015.
